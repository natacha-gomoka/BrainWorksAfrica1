CEO

Noboru Kondo

Born in Tokushima Prefecture in 1962. Representative director of Brain Works Co., Ltd. He graduated from the Department of Architecture, Faculty of Engineering, Kobe University. He is qualified as a first-class architect and special information processing engineer. He mainly works as a management helper for small and medium-sized enterprises, but also supports organizational activities of companies, government offices, local governments, etc. Meanwhile, he has also taken on the challenge of Asian business, and is now familiar with Southeast Asian business, with a focus on Vietnam. In particular, we have a large track record of supporting business creation and business innovation in emerging countries. He is currently involved in business activities in Africa and India. His motto is to focus on the credit business, which is one of Japan's strengths. His representative books include “ What if Namihira was 77 years old? ” and “ What if the president of my company was an AI? ”
